## R Scripts

This folder contains the R scripts that were utilized when conducting this research.

- `0_data_cleaning.R`: cleaning and preparation of raw ATP dat
- `1_initial_setup.R`: initial data split & forming of resamples
- `1.5_EDA.R`: EDA of minutes variable
- `2_recipe_development.R`: data preprocessing/feature engineering for various models
- `3.0_null.R`: fitting of null models to resamples 
- `3.1_lm.R`: fitting of linear regression models to resamples 
- `3.2_boosted_tree.R`: fitting of boosted tree models to resamples 
- `3.3_elastic_net.R`: fitting of elastic net models to resamples 
- `3.4_knn.R`: fitting of k-nearest neighbor models to resamples 
- `3.5_rf.R`: fitting of random forest models to resamples 
- `4_model_analysis_ex1.R`: analysis/comparison of models fit to resamples, final model selection
- `5_final_model_ex1.R`: training/fitting of final model, analysis of its performance
