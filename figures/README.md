## Figures

All of the figures were developed following analysis of the ATP match data.

- `cor_matrix.png`: correlation matrix showing the correlation of numerical variables in the cleaned dataset.
- `level_duration_boxplot.png`: boxplot showing the categorical relationship of the tournament level and match duration
- `minutes_histogram.png`: histogram showing distribution of minutes of ATP matches.
- `minutes_plot.png`: scatterplot showing relationship of actual and predicted minutes for the final model.
- `ponts_games.png`: scatterplot showing relationship of service points and service games for matches.
- `surface_duration_boxplot.png`: boxplot showing the categorical relationship of the match surface and match duration
